Core Features

A CONVENIENT ORDER APP

Availability of hundreds of restaurants to choose from, a hassle-free order process. Cancel any time.

SEAMLESS DELIVERY APP

Fulfill received orders fast. With the functionality to autoassign orders to every member involved in the order fulfillment system.
