Let us walk you through our

PROCESS

TECHSPIRE STUDIOS endeavors to get your projects completed with complete transparency and surety. We focus on developing a mutual understanding while taking on the responsibility of your project. Our project managers and support representatives are reachable 24/7 to assist you in all matters concerning your project.

Know Where Your Project is Headed

01

Protecting
your
information

Your privacy supersedes everything -
 hence, we get started with an NDA.
This ensures:

  • SAFEGUARDING YOUR SENSITIVE INFORMATION,
  • INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY PROTECTION,
  • SECURING ALL PROJECT-RELATED INFORMATION.

02

Analyzing
your
idea

Our Business and Technical Analysts will continue to assess your requirements. Following that, we draft a Scope of Work (SoW), and a Level of Effort (LoE) document. We do this to describe the project flow and list the components involved in product development.

03

Cost
Estimation

Here, we estimate the total cost after reviewing the essential elements required to include in your product – getting us ready to start the project. This also helps you get a clearer picture of how your budget will be utilized.

04

Expanding
your
idea

Finalizing the budget is followed by dedicating an account manager to your project. In this step, we'll envision the endless possibilities to set us in the right direction. Most of the brainstorming and blueprinting are done here.

05

Preparing
UI / UX Designs
+ other blueprints

Before Product Development, workshopping and preparing UI/UX designs is crucial. Originality is what Techspire Studios From basic design elements to complex UI designs, all is pure and original.

06

Development
with THE AGILE MANIFESTO

As the principles of Agile Software Development suggests, your satisfaction is foremost. Our development teams are always accessible and ready to cater to your requirements even in later development stages. We deliver working software concepts weekly, so you don't have to keep waiting to know where your project is headed.

07

Test
your
product

Concluding the development process is followed by the extensive testing of your product. The Quality Assurance team employs multiple testing approaches: functional testing, integrational testing, and usability testing. Using these, and other smart testing methodologies, a perfect product is guaranteed.

08

Technical
support

Once your project has reached completion, we direct our focus to providing you with convenient tech support and maintenance. Techspire Studios
