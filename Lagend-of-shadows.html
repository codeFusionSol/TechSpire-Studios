About TACO BOSS

TACO BOSS has aimed to create a runner game with no boundaries. Download for free from App Store or Play Store and start playing. We want players to earn in-game benefits from their performance, not with purchases. Plus, you can customize your game levels and vehicles however you want.

TACO BOSS has been developed to reinvent excitement in runner platform games. With 3D graphics and customizable platforms and game elements, there is so much in store for all players.

TACO BOSS Highlights

TACO BOSS brings a new experience in the runner platform game niche. Including options to maneuver your vehicle in multiple dimensions, this exhilarating 3D gameplay will keep you on the edge of your seat.

CHALLENGING WITH EVERY LEVEL

Your chosen Taco van will get increased speed with every level. Making the gameplay more and more challenging. TACO BOSS also gives you various power-ups to keep up with the increasing challenges.

WIDE GAMING COMMUNITY

TACO BOSS has a wide community of players who are enthusiastic to improve the gameplay for everyone. We love to stay transparent and include as many players in our beta program.

CUSTOMIZABLE GAMEPLAY

TACO BOSS allows you to challenge yourself like never before. Apart from customizing your favorite vans, the game also provides the freedom to customize entire levels.

MULTIPLAYER GAMEPLAY

After setting common game parameters, create custom challenges and play with friends. Connect with your friends on social platforms and keep track of their performance.

Regular Testing

Our gaming community is excited to work with Techspire Studios's development team to improve the game experience. Our beta program is open to anyone passionate about TACO BOSS.
